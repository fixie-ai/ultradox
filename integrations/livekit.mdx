<Note>
[LiveKit](https://livekit.com/) provides a voice platform that runs in a fully managed cloud or can be self-hosted. It offers
a native Ultravox integration that makes it easy to use Ultravox with your voice agents.
</Note>

See the [LiveKit documentation](https://docs.livekit.io/agents/models/realtime/plugins/ultravox/) for more information.
